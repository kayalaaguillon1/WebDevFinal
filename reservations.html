Parking Information

There is a main lot on the east side of the restaurant where you can park your vehicle, and parking on the street is available by meter. If you place a reservation ahead of time, we may be able to offer you valet service. (Valet availability can be checked by calling after placing a reservation) Tipping is not required, but encouraged. If choosing the valet service, alert your server about 5-10 minutes before you choose to leave, and your vehicle will be waiting for you in front of the building.
